What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive plumbing solutions for homes and businesses across Cape Town.

Leak Repairs

Fast detection and repair of leaks in pipes, taps, and fixtures. We minimise water damage and get things flowing properly again.

Bathroom Plumbing

Full installations and renovations — from toilets and basins to showers and baths. Clean, professional workmanship every time.

Geyser Services

Installation, repair, and maintenance of electric and solar geysers. Stay compliant and never run out of hot water.

Pipe Replacement

Old, corroded, or burst pipe upgrades using quality materials built to last. We handle copper, PVC, and PEX systems.

Drain Cleaning

Blocked drains cleared fast with professional equipment. We tackle kitchens, bathrooms, and outdoor drains.

Emergency Plumbing

24/7 emergency callout for burst pipes, flooding, and sewage backups. Fast response when you need it most.

Our Story

About Smith Plumbing

Founded in 2015 by John Smith, a master plumber with 15 years of experience, Smith Plumbing has grown from a one-man operation into Cape Town's most trusted plumbing service.

What sets us apart is our commitment to transparent pricing and clean workmanship. We treat your home like our own — and we always leave it cleaner than we found it.

  • Fully licensed & insured
  • Transparent, upfront pricing
  • Same-day service available
  • 5-year workmanship guarantee
